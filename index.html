About GKC

Based in Birmingham, Alabama, Grace Klein Community is a 501(c)3 non-profit that focuses on changing the world by teaching people how to truly share with one another.

We strive to build close relationships that mean something and that can encourage and spur people on to be better.

Ways We Serve

Give and Take Room

+

Almost every Monday the Grace Klein Community front porch is filled with donations from the previous weekend. Pretty much every single day someone shows up to give something. Generosity is clearly understood in the concept of the Give and Take Room.

Food Delivery

+

Our monthly food delivery ministry is a great way to get involved in the Birmingham community. Our volunteers go out, once a month, to deliver food boxes to families in need. We do this to build relationships with people and walk through hard times with them.

Homecooked Meals

+

In 2015, a volunteer had prepared 13 meals for various family situations and delivered 10 of them. Situations have included: recovery from surgeries, death of a parent, and financial distress.

Our Mission

is to create authentic community (Koinonia) by uniting diverse individuals, businesses, ministries, and churches to work together by sharing what they have in order to meet physical and spiritual needs both locally and globally.

Our Vision

is to show people that they are deeply valued and that we all have something to give.

Our Purpose

is to demonstrate the love of Christ.
